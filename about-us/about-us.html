About Us

DOATOONs is an animation studio dedicated to telling powerful stories that transcend age, culture, and language.

We create high-quality animations that inspire, entertain, and educate, making animation a universal language.

What sets us apart is our commitment to empowering young creatives—the term, 'DOAT' stands for Directive Organization Accomplished by Teenagers. We believe in the fresh perspectives of teenage artists, giving them a platform to shape the future of animation.

From a starting budget of £0.00, DOATOONs has grown through pure creativity and passion.

Join us on this journey as we continue to push the boundaries of animation, connecting people across the globe.
